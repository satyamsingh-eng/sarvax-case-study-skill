# SARVAX ICP Profiles

## ICP 1 — Staffing & Recruitment Agency (East Africa / MENA)
- **Size:** 15–80 employees
- **Pain:** High volume hiring, lean HR team, manual screening
- **Agent:** HR Agent
- **Decision Maker:** Founder, Head of Talent
- **ROI Frame:** Faster placements = more revenue per recruiter

## ICP 2 — B2B SaaS (South Asia / Africa)
- **Size:** 30–200 employees
- **Pain:** Small SDR team, low outreach volume, inconsistent follow-up
- **Agent:** Sales Agent
- **Decision Maker:** Sales Director, Head of Growth, CEO
- **ROI Frame:** More pipeline with same team size

## ICP 3 — Professional Services / Consulting (Global)
- **Size:** 20–150 employees
- **Pain:** Ops overhead, manual reporting, coordination drag
- **Agent:** Ops Agent
- **Decision Maker:** COO, Operations Manager
- **ROI Frame:** Leadership time recovered, faster delivery cycles

## ICP 4 — Logistics / Distribution (MENA / East Africa)
- **Size:** 50–500 employees
- **Pain:** Manual coordination, workforce scheduling, status reporting
- **Agent:** Ops Agent + HR Agent
- **Decision Maker:** Operations Director, HR Manager
- **ROI Frame:** Reduced coordination cost, faster workforce deployment

## Qualification Signals
- Team doing repetitive manual work at scale
- Growth stalled by operational bandwidth
- Hiring more people to solve process problems
- Decision makers frustrated with visibility / control
- Currently using spreadsheets, WhatsApp, or basic tools for ops
