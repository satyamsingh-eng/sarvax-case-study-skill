## Who SARVAX Is

SARVAX deploys AI agents that take ownership of entire business functions -
not just tools that assist employees. When a company deploys a SARVAX agent,
that function becomes autonomous. The team oversees outcomes; the agent handles
execution.

**Core promise:** Your operations run. Your team scales without headcount.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary:**
- B2B companies, 20-500 employees
- Experiencing operational bottlenecks due to manual processes
- Hiring to solve problems that should be automated
- Departments: HR, Sales Ops, Finance Ops, Customer Success Ops
- Geography: East Africa (Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania), Gulf (UAE, KSA), UK SME

**Pain signatures:**
- "We need to hire someone to manage X"
- "The team spends too much time on Y"
- "We can't scale without more staff"
- "Our process breaks when someone is out"

**Not a fit:**
- Pure B2C companies
- Companies needing consumer-facing AI features
- Enterprises with existing deep automation stacks

## SARVAX Agents

### HR Agent
**Function:** End-to-end hiring and talent operations
**Takes over:** Job posting, CV screening, candidate ranking, interview scheduling,
offer letter generation, onboarding documentation
**Eliminates:** Manual CV review, calendar coordination, recruiter admin load
**Best for:** Companies hiring 5+ roles per quarter with 2-person HR teams

### Sales Operations Agent
**Function:** Lead qualification, pipeline management, follow-up sequencing
**Takes over:** Lead scoring, CRM data entry, follow-up scheduling, deal stage updates
**Eliminates:** Manual pipeline hygiene, forgotten follow-ups, inconsistent lead scoring
**Best for:** Sales teams of 3-15 reps without a dedicated RevOps function

### Customer Success Agent
**Function:** Onboarding, health scoring, renewal management
**Takes over:** Onboarding task coordination, check-in scheduling, health score tracking,
renewal alert generation
**Eliminates:** Manual check-in tracking, missed renewal signals, inconsistent onboarding
**Best for:** SaaS or service companies with 50-500 managed accounts

### Finance Operations Agent
**Function:** Invoicing, payment follow-up, expense processing
**Takes over:** Invoice generation, payment reminder sequences, expense categorisation,
reconciliation flagging
**Eliminates:** Manual follow-up on overdue invoices, finance admin backlog
**Best for:** Companies with 100+ monthly invoices and a 1-2 person finance team

### Operations Coordinator Agent
**Function:** Cross-department process coordination
**Takes over:** Status updates, task assignment, deadline tracking, report generation
**Eliminates:** Manual coordination between departments, status meetings, spreadsheet tracking
**Best for:** Operations teams managing multiple departments or projects simultaneously
